Our Story

The Farms at Solivera is a medium-scale cocoa and palm farm located in Bong County, Liberia (7°3'3" N 9°46'43" W). Since our inception in January 2022, we have been dedicated to producing high-quality cocoa and palm products for export to the international market. We operate on 324 acres of land, committed to sustainable agricultural practices that ensure our expansion has minimal environmental impact.

Our farm is powered by a 5 KWH solar system, providing 24/7 clean energy, and we have a borehole supplying safe drinking water, significantly improving the lives of our workers. We prioritize gender inclusivity, with a goal to have at least 50% of our workforce comprised of women, and we actively promote women in leadership positions.

Looking ahead, we plan to build more housing, increase our renewable electricity generation, and bore additional wells to support other parts of the farm. At The Farms at Solivera, we are not just growing crops; we are nurturing a sustainable and inclusive community.
