The Farms at Solivera

Welcome to our farm nestled approximately 10 miles (16 kilometers) southwest of the breathtaking Kpatawee Waterfalls in Bong County, Liberia. Our farm is situated in the lush and fertile landscapes of this region, offering an ideal environment for cultivating high-quality cocoa beans & Palm products.

Join us on a journey of discovery as we celebrate the beauty of Bong County and the artistry of cocoa farming. Experience the magic of our farm, where the rhythm of nature and the passion of our farmers converge to (soon) create cocoa beans and palmns products of unparalleled quality and flavor.
